 Gameplay Features:
  1. Hero Skills/Abilities - Active/passive skills that unlock at certain levels, affecting quests
  2. Random Events - Encounters during quests (ambushes, treasures, merchants)
  3. Guild Facilities - Buildable structures (Training Hall, Forge, Library) that provide bonuses
  4. Hero Bonds/Relationships - Heroes who quest together gain synergy bonuses

  Meta Features:
  5. Save/Load System - Persist progress between sessions
  6. Achievements - Unlock rewards for milestones
  7. Prestige/New Game+ - Reset with bonuses after reaching certain goals

  Quality of Life:
  8. Sound & Music - Audio feedback and atmosphere
  9. Tutorial/Tooltips - Help new players understand systems
  10. Quest Auto-Assignment - Let the game pick optimal parties
